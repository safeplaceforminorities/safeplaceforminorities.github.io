What is a safe space?

A safe place is a place where you visit and no one judges you. It is where you can be who you truly are and comfortable, where everyone accepts who you are and the diversity you bring.



A place to talk

Our chatroom offers our visitors a place to talk to a counselor about experiences that they have had. The counselor will help answer complex questions and give light to complicated and unfair situations along with providing solutions.



Who we are

We are three young women. We met at girls who code and we have seen the cruel reality of how minorities are treated. I, Rhomin, was the one who came up with the idea. Lauren and Luciana have worked alongside me tirelessly to make this project a reality. We are proud of what we've achieved and hope that this or even the idea of this could be helpful to those who are open to it.
